About

I don't just build products—I build them, ship them, and keep them running. Product thinking, technical execution, and operations, end to end.

At N26, I owned payments infrastructure for 3M+ users—incident response, PSD2 compliance, Apple Pay and Google Pay launches across Europe. Promoted four times in two years. At Urban Sports Club, I led payments, platform engineering, and site reliability: fraud detection (92% reduction, €2.2M saved), cloud migrations, legacy system upgrades, and the kind of debugging where money goes missing and someone has to find it.

Now I build custom platforms for businesses that need the whole thing—product strategy, architecture, implementation, monitoring, and ongoing operations. From concept to production in weeks, not months.

Recent work spans healthcare platforms, e-commerce infrastructure, and practitioner management systems. I'm selective about what I take on—I work on projects that empower people, in domains where getting it wrong has real consequences.
